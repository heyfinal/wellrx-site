§ I · The Charter

WellRX is enrolling two small-to-mid-range gas-well operators as charter partners.

The charter-partner program is a structured three-month beta engagement designed to stress-test the WellRX diagnostic engine against a second and third live SCADA environment while delivering genuine decision-support value to the operator from day one. Charter partners receive material concessions on setup and subscription in exchange for engaged feedback and case-study rights at program close.

Charter Partner Concessions

  • Setup fee waived in full. Standard onboarding — adapter build, credentialing, historical backfill, and kickoff — is performed at no cost to charter partners.
  • Portfolio diagnostic included. A board-grade review of every active well in your portfolio, with case-by-case diagnosis, RTU health, chemistry program assessment, artificial-lift strategy, and a 90-day action plan with confidence-scored ROI. Normally quoted separately.
  • Prior-quarter retrospective included. A 90-day look-back against your historical data: what was trending, what was missed, what was worth chasing. Delivered alongside the portfolio diagnostic at program kickoff.
  • 50% off the monthly subscription for the first three months — the beta window itself. Concession applies against the package rate quoted to you.
Package is still quoted & bid per specification. Rate scales with report types enrolled, daily/weekly report volume, number of tailored-report recipients, and integration complexity of your data sources. Supplemental AI agents (reservoir, midstream, HSE, regulatory, allocation, custom-trained) are priced per-agent to cover incremental model-API consumption.

Who we're looking for

  • Small-to-mid-range operator (roughly 20–150 active wells)
  • Live production-data stream — SCADA, historian, API, SFTP, or structured exports
  • A designated technical champion — preferably IT or engineering — available for working calls during the beta
  • Authority to approve a named anonymized case study at program close

What you get from day one

  • Every active well reviewed by the virtual AI petro + production + chemist team
  • Role-tailored daily/weekly Rx delivered to the recipients you specify
  • Direct support line for engineering feedback and adjustments during the beta
  • Post-beta: continue at quoted rate or wind down with zero penalty
