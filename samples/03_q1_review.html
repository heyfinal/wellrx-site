Program Summary & Q2 Recommendations

Three moves going into Q2. Sorted by cost-of-delay — velocity-string re-dose is the cheapest and highest-confidence win.

1 · Re-dose the velocity-string cohort to target. Brenner 4/5, Holloway 1, GH Winslow 4, Pine Deephole, Trevino 10, Mansfield 8 — all showing 20%+ decline vs Q1 with chemical rate 10-25% under target. Bumping the foamer 0.5-1.0 gpd on each is ~$300/well/month incremental spend against ~$1,200/well/month recoverable revenue. Confidence: high.

2 · FSR-audit the plunger cohort. Lyman 3/4, Jessica Crockett, Hawthorne Meridian 4/11, GH Winslow 5, Wesley 5/6, Virginia Garland 2, Clement 4, Halsey 2 — all within 5% of Q1 average (healthy) but FSR records are stale. Lock in a quarterly audit cadence so we know what target is actually being delivered at site.

3 · Evaluate Lyman 5 / Brenner 4 / Wolford 5 for lift conversion. These three show -45% vs Q1 with water rising 40%+ — classic plunger-falling-behind signature. Pull a plunger arrival log (or BHP if available) and scope a velocity-string trial on the worst of them. Rough capex: $8-12k/well installed. Payback: 3-6 months at observed deltas.
